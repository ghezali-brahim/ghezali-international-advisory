<h2>Regulatory Framework for Cross-Border Expansion in the US</h2>
<p>The US regulatory landscape includes federal and state layers. CFIUS reviews foreign investment in sensitive sectors. Antitrust (FTC, DOJ) applies to mergers above thresholds. Sector-specific regulators (FDA, SEC, etc.) apply where relevant. Employment law varies by state. Our cross-border expansion consulting includes early assessment of regulatory applicability and coordination with specialist counsel.</p>
<p>Tax structuring is critical. The US has a complex federal and state tax system. Treaty networks, holding structures, and deal economics must be aligned. We work with clients and their tax advisers to optimise structures while maintaining operational flexibility. Understanding US GAAP and reporting requirements is essential for investors and portfolio companies.</p>
